Single Speed Drive Train

How to use:

Read the document, it should explain how a single speed drive train works. If you would like to see what effect parameters of the motor or your transmission have on the output you can simple click and drag any of the blue numbers to adjust them. Enjoy!

I have a motor that runs at revolutions per minute (rpm) at volts. It is currently running at volts which means that the motor is actually running at rpm The spec sheet says that the stall torque is Newton-meters and the motor will draw amps. With no load on the motor it will draw amps.
